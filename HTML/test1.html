MAGGI

Ingredients

  • maggi packet
  • oil
  • some veggies
  • maggi masala
  • some spicies

Recipe

  1. take a pan
  2. add oil to it, when the oil is hot add cumin seeds and chillies
  3. after that add chop veggies
  4. when the veggies are cooked add salt,red chilli powder,turmeric powder and maggi masala
  5. after few minutes add water to it
  6. when water starts boiling add maggi
  7. let the maggi cook
  8. serve hot
  9. ENJOY!!
